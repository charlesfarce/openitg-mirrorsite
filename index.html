OpenITG

Installs | Upgrade Packs | References | Logs | Archive

OpenITG is an open-source rhythm dancing game which is a fork of StepMania 3.95 with the goal of adding arcade-like ITG-style behavior and serving as a drop-in replacement for the ITG binary on arcade cabinents.

DISCLAIMER: This is a completely unofficial build of StepMania and is not backed by Konami, RoXoR, etc. or employees/subsidiaries thereof.
